Holiday Pumpkin Pie

Holiday Pumpkin Pie by Odin Recipes

A velvety smooth filling made with pumpkin puree, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, all nestled in a flaky, buttery crust. Pumpkin pie is a fall favorite, ideal for Thanksgiving or any gathering that calls for a slice of cozy sweetness!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust (or homemade if preferred)
  • 2 cups canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Prepare the filling: In a large bowl, whisk together pumpkin puree, heavy cream, eggs, sugar, spices, salt, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Assemble the pie: Pour the pumpkin mixture into the prepared pie crust.
  4. Bake: Bake for 15 minutes at 425°F, then lower the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for an additional 40-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Cool: Let the pie cool completely on a wire rack before serving.
  6. Serve: Top with whipped cream or enjoy as is!
