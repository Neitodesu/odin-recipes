Holiday Cheesecake

Holiday Cheesecake by Odin Recipes

This creamy cheesecake features a buttery graham cracker crust and a smooth, rich filling made from cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Baked gently for a silky texture, it’s perfect on its own or topped with fresh fruit or chocolate. A simple yet indulgent treat for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (optional, for extra creaminess)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Make the crust: In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove and set aside.
  3. Prepare the filling: Beat the cream cheese and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Assemble the cheesecake: Pour the cream cheese mixture over the cooled crust in the springform pan.
  5. Bake: Place the cheesecake in the oven and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is slightly jiggly.
  6. Cool: Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour. Then transfer to the fridge and chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Serve: Top with fresh fruit, chocolate, or your favorite topping, and enjoy!
