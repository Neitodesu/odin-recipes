Holiday Apple Pie by Odin Recipes

This classic apple pie features a flaky, buttery crust filled with a sweet and spiced apple mixture. With cinnamon, nutmeg, and a hint of lemon, every bite is warm and comforting. Perfect for any occasion, it’s great on its own or served with vanilla ice cream for an extra treat!

Ingredients

  • -2 pre-made pie crusts (or homemade if preferred)
  • -6 cups peeled and sliced apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work well)
  • -3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • -1/4 cup brown sugar
  • -1 tbsp lemon juice
  • -1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • -1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • -2 tbsp all-purpose flour (for thickening)
  • -1 tbsp butter (cut into small pieces)

Steps

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. 2. Prepare the filling: In a bowl, combine apples, sugars, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and flour. Toss until apples are well coated.
  3. 3. Assemble the pie: Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie pan. Fill with the apple mixture and dot with butter. Place the second crust over the apples and crimp the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
  4. 4. Bake: Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  5. 5. Cool: Let the pie cool for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. 6. Serve: Enjoy as is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream!
